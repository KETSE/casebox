Ext Spinner Widget for ExtJS 2.x

Go to the forum for more information on its usage and support.

Example
Spinner applied to textfield
  • Click the control to spin.
  • Hold "Shift" for alternate spinning.
  • Drag the divider with your mouse to spin.
  • Put focus on field and press "up" or "down" to spin.
  • When focused press "pageUp" or "pageDown" for alternate spinning.
  • Hover the control and use your scrollwheel on your mouse to spin.
